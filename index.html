Our Story

Cozy Cafe began as a small espresso cart in the heart of Osaka, Japan. Inspired by slow mornings and meaningful conversations, we’ve grown into a cozy café known for our handcrafted brews and inviting atmosphere.

Our founders believed that coffee is more than just a drink — it’s a moment, a pause, a connection. Today, COZY CAFE continues to celebrate that spirit by sourcing premium beans from around the world, blending tradition with modern coffee culture.

Whether you're here for your daily pick-me-up or a place to unwind with friends, we welcome you with open arms — and a warm cup of coffee.
